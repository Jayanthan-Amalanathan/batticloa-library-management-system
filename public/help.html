FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I become a library member?
Visit the membership registration page to apply online. You will need a valid government ID and proof of address. Once approved you will receive a confirmation email. Approval usually takes 1 to 2 business days.
How many books can I borrow at one time?
Loan limits by membership category: Students 3 items, Public Adults 5 items, Researchers 10 items, Children 2 items.
What is the loan period and can I renew?
Standard loan is 14 days for general lending and 21 days for fiction. Renewals can be done online via your member dashboard or in person, up to two times, as long as no other member has reserved the item.
What are the late return fines?
LKR 10 per day per item. Fines accrue automatically and can be paid at the circulation desk. Borrowing privileges are suspended at LKR 500 outstanding. Fines are waived for senior citizens and persons with disabilities.
How do I use the catalog search?
Our catalog lets you search the local collection by title, author, ISBN, subject, or call number. Use quotes for exact phrases, e.g. "one hundred years". You can also filter by language, category, and availability. Try it now →
Can I access digital resources from home?
Yes. Active members can log in to access e-books, audiobooks, online databases, and digitized newspapers from any device. Member login →
How do I reserve a book that's currently on loan?
Search for the title in the catalog, then click Reserve on its listing. You'll be added to the hold queue and notified by email or SMS when the item is available for pickup.
Do you offer Wi-Fi and computer access?
Yes. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout all branches for members. We also have 20+ public-access computers at the main branch. Booking is recommended; contact the front desk or use your member dashboard.
How do I volunteer or donate books?
We welcome volunteers for storytime assistance, digitization projects, and event support. Book and financial donations are accepted. Receipts are provided for all contributions. Contact us → to get started.
