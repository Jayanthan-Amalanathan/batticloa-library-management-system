FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I become a library member?
Visit the membership registration page to apply online. You'll need a valid government ID and proof of address. Approval typically takes 1–2 business days, and you'll receive a confirmation email when your membership is active.
How many books can I borrow at one time?
Loan limits depend on your membership category:
Students: 3 items  ·  Public Adults: 5 items  ·  Researchers: 10 items  ·  Children: 2 items
What is the loan period and can I renew?
Standard loan is 14 days for general lending and 21 days for fiction. Renewals can be done online via your member dashboard or in-person, up to two times — provided no holds are queued on the item.
What are the late return fines?
LKR 10 per day per item. Fines accrue automatically and can be paid at the circulation desk. Borrowing privileges are suspended at LKR 500 outstanding. Fines are waived for senior citizens and persons with disabilities.
How do I use the Koha OPAC / catalog search?
Our integrated Koha OPAC searches across Batticaloa Public Library, Eastern University Library and partner libraries in one query. Enter keywords, or choose Author / Title / ISBN / Subject / Series / Call Number from the dropdown for precise results. Use quotes for exact phrases, e.g. "one hundred years". Try it now →
Can I access digital resources from home?
Yes! Active members can log in to access e-books, audiobooks, online databases, and digitized newspapers from anywhere with an internet connection. Member login →
How do I reserve a book that's currently on loan?
Search for the title in the catalog, then click Reserve on its listing. You'll be added to the hold queue and notified by email or SMS when the item is available for pickup.
Do you offer Wi-Fi and computer access?
Yes. Free high-speed Wi-Fi is available throughout all branches for members. We also have 20+ public-access computers at the main branch — booking recommended via the front desk or your member dashboard.
How do I volunteer or donate books?
We welcome volunteers for storytime assistance, digitization projects, and event support. Book donations and financial contributions are also gratefully received — tax-deductible receipts provided. Contact us → to get started.
What is the Eastern University catalog integration?
Since 2018, our library system has been integrated with the Eastern University Library at Vantharumoolai via the Koha ILS. This means a single OPAC search covers both catalogs. Items from Eastern University can be requested via inter-library loan for researcher-grade members.
