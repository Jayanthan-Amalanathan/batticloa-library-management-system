How to Apply

Three Steps to Membership

Most applications are processed the same day.

1

Gather Documents

Collect your application form, tax receipt, National ID or Passport, and your guarantor's tax receipt.

2

Visit the Library

Bring all documents to the library counter during opening hours. Staff will verify and process your application.

3

Collect Your Card

Pay the registration fee, receive your membership card, and start borrowing immediately.

Eligibility

Open to all residents within the city limits. Proof of address and identity are required. Applicants must provide a guarantor residing within the city limits.

Suspension & Cancellation

The library may suspend or cancel membership for non-payment of fines, repeated rule violations, or misconduct. Suspended members may appeal to the Head Librarian within 30 days.
