Our Mission

A Modern Knowledge Hub for Batticaloa

For over five decades, the Batticaloa Public Library has served as the cultural and intellectual heart of the Eastern Province. Today, we are transforming into a fully digital, integrated library — connecting Koha catalogs, the Eastern University Library, and Open Library to bring the world's knowledge to every citizen.

  • Integrated catalog spanning multiple libraries
  • Multilingual services in English, Tamil, and Sinhala
  • Digital archive of local heritage and rare manuscripts
  • Free programs for students, seniors, and researchers
  • Accessible to all — WCAG 2.1 compliant platform
