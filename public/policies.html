Lending & Borrowing Policy

The Batticaloa Public Library lends materials to all active members in good standing. The following rules and regulations apply:

Borrowing Limits

A member may borrow a maximum of three books at a time.

Loan Period & Fines

Loan PeriodFine (per day, per book)
14 daysRs. 5.00

All borrowed materials may be kept for a period of 14 days. A fine of Rs. 5.00 per day per book will be collected for overdue items.

Extension of Borrowing Period

If a member requires a book for more than 14 days, the borrowing period may be extended once for an additional 14 days. In such a case the member should inform the library in person or by telephone before the due date.

Lost or Damaged Items

In the event that a book is lost or damaged by a member, a charge equivalent to three times the value of the book, together with a binding fee of Rs. 100.00, will be imposed.

Reservations (Holds)

Members may place holds on items currently on loan. Reserved items are held for 5 business days after notification before being returned to the shelf.

Membership Policy

How to Obtain Library Membership

Library membership may be obtained by submitting a duly completed membership application form together with a photocopy of the tax receipt confirming that the applicant's address is within the city limits.

In addition, the applicant must be guaranteed by a guarantor residing within the city limits, and a photocopy of the guarantor's tax receipt must also be submitted along with the application.

Registration Fee

CategoryRegistration FeeAnnual Renewal Fee
AdultsRs. 300.00Rs. 100.00
Children (below 14 years)Rs. 100.00Rs. 50.00

Upon registration, a membership identity card will be issued. Membership must be renewed annually.

Eligibility

Membership is open to all residents within the city limits. Proof of address (tax receipt) and identity (National Identity Card, Passport, or equivalent) are required at registration.

Change of Guarantor

In the event of a change of guarantor, a copy of the new guarantor's tax receipt must be submitted.

Suspension & Cancellation

The library reserves the right to suspend or cancel membership for non-payment of fines, repeated violation of borrowing rules, or misconduct on library premises. Suspended members may appeal to the Head Librarian within 30 days.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 January 2025

Information We Collect

  • Registration data: full name, email, phone, address, date of birth, NIC and membership category.
  • Loan and reservation records: which materials you borrow, when and for how long.
  • System usage data: login timestamps and IP addresses for security auditing.
  • Chat messages: messages sent via the Library Assistant are stored for 90 days and are only accessible to library staff.
  • Contact form messages: name, email and message content when you contact us.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to administer library services, communicate membership status, enforce borrowing policies and generate anonymised statistical reports. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except as required by Sri Lankan law.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. To make a request, contact the library at privacy@batticaloalibrary.lk or visit in person with photo ID. We will respond within 30 days.

Cookies & Local Storage

This website uses a single HTTP-only session cookie for authentication and browser localStorage to remember your language preference. No advertising or tracking cookies are used.

Intellectual Freedom

The library protects patron confidentiality. Borrowing records are confidential and will not be disclosed to any party, including law enforcement, without a valid court order.

Website Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 January 2025

This policy explains how the Batticaloa Public Library website (batticaloalibrary.lk) collects, uses, and protects information when you visit or interact with it. It applies to all website visitors, whether or not you hold a library membership.

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit this website, our web server automatically records the following technical data for security and performance purposes:

  • IP address and approximate geographic region (country/city level).
  • Browser type, version, and operating system.
  • Pages visited, time spent on each page, and navigation path.
  • Date and time of each visit.
  • Referring URL (the page you came from, if any).
  • HTTP response codes and error logs.

This data is not linked to your identity unless you are logged in as a registered member, in which case your member ID may be attached to server logs for audit purposes.

Information You Provide

  • Account registration: name, email address, phone number, and address when you register online.
  • Contact form: name, email, and message when you submit an enquiry.
  • Library Assistant (chat): messages sent are temporarily stored to provide the service and improve responses.
  • Search queries: catalogue searches entered on the website for the purpose of returning results.

Session cookie (HTTP-only)Keeps you logged in securelyUntil browser is closed or logout

We do not use advertising cookies, cross-site tracking, or third-party analytics scripts. You can delete cookies and localStorage data at any time through your browser settings; doing so will log you out and reset language preferences.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Fonts: Font files are loaded from Google's CDN. Google may record your IP address when serving these files. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Hostinger (hosting): The website is hosted on Hostinger's VPS infrastructure, which processes request logs. See Hostinger's Privacy Policy.

No other third-party services (social media embeds, advertising networks, or analytics platforms) are used on this website.

How We Use Website Data

  • To operate and maintain the library's online services.
  • To authenticate your account and keep your session secure.
  • To diagnose technical errors and improve website performance.
  • To detect and prevent unauthorised access or abuse.
  • To respond to enquiries submitted through the contact form or chat.

We do not sell, rent, or share your website usage data with any third party for commercial purposes.

Data Security

All data transmitted between your browser and this website is encrypted using HTTPS (TLS). Passwords are stored using a one-way cryptographic hash and are never stored in plain text. Access to user data is restricted to authorised library staff only.

Links to Other Websites

This website may contain links to external sites (e.g., government portals, partner organisations). We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

Children's Privacy

Children under the age of 14 may only register for a library account with the consent of a parent or guardian. We do not knowingly collect personal data directly from children without parental consent. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us immediately.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Request deletion of your data, subject to any legal retention obligations.
  • Withdraw consent for any processing that relies on consent.
  • Opt out of non-essential data collection by adjusting your browser settings.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@batticaloalibrary.lk or visit the library in person with valid photo identification. We will respond within 30 days.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Website Privacy Policy periodically. The "Last updated" date at the top of this section will reflect when changes were made. Continued use of the website after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy. For significant changes, we will post a notice on the library's homepage.

Contact

For any questions or concerns regarding this Website Privacy Policy, please contact the library at privacy@batticaloalibrary.lk, call 065-2222484, or write to: Batticaloa Public Library, Librarian Road, Batticaloa 30000, Sri Lanka.

Computer & Internet Use Policy

The library provides free public computer and Wi-Fi access to all members and visitors. By using library technology resources, you agree to the following terms:

  • Sessions are limited to 60 minutes per day when others are waiting.
  • Priority access is given to members working on academic or research tasks.
  • Viewing, downloading, or sharing illegal, offensive, or harmful content is strictly prohibited.
  • Users may not attempt to bypass library content filters or engage in hacking activities.
  • Personal data must not be left on library computers. Always log out of all accounts before leaving.
  • Commercial use (running a business from library computers) is not permitted without prior approval.
  • Printing is available at LKR 5 per black-and-white page, LKR 15 per colour page.

Code of Conduct

All library users are expected to treat staff, other patrons, and library property with respect. The following behaviours are not permitted:

  • Disruptive noise. Please use quiet voices in reading areas.
  • Harassment, discrimination, or threatening behaviour toward any person.
  • Vandalism or damage to library property or materials.
  • Eating or drinking near computer terminals or in reading rooms (designated café area excepted).
  • Smoking or vaping anywhere on library premises.
  • Soliciting, hawking, or canvassing without written permission from the library director.

Violations may result in removal from the premises and, for serious or repeated offences, suspension or cancellation of membership. Decisions may be appealed to the Head Librarian within 14 days.

Accessibility Statement (WCAG 2.1)

Batticaloa Public Library works to make its digital platform accessible to all users, including people with disabilities. We aim to meet the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA.

Measures Taken

  • Semantic HTML5 elements and ARIA landmarks throughout the site.
  • All images include descriptive alt text.
  • Colour contrast ratios meet AA requirements for all text elements.
  • All interactive elements are reachable by keyboard navigation.
  • Focus indicators are clearly visible.
  • Support for browser zoom up to 200% without horizontal scrolling.
  • Multilingual support in English, Tamil (தமிழ்), and Sinhala (සිංහල).

Physical Accessibility

The main library building features ramp access, accessible restrooms, and designated seating for patrons with disabilities. Large-print books and screen-reader compatible terminals are available on request.

Feedback

If you experience any accessibility barriers, please contact us at access@batticaloalibrary.lk or call 065-2222484 / 077-6718944. We aim to resolve accessibility concerns within 5 business days.
